Heat Maps for Campaign Activation

Visualize thousands of data points to ascertain focus areas for on ground activity.

Ooh | Jan 21 2021
By Guru Cingh

Heatmap is quite an intuitive data visualization technique. Especially in a scenario where a lot of decision making is required,e.g. while creating an Out-of-Home media plan for a consumer product targeted to a certain demographic population residing in certain geographic areas.

Heatmaps uncomplicate decision making on our planning platform If you look at the image below, it clearly depicts activation point for pharma or related product.

It simply does not get simpler than that.

The planner just has to find the relevant areas, create a mandate for the geography and forward it to the media agency for media site availabilities and other related operations.
