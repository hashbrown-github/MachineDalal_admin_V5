Features at a Glance

Smart Maps

Maps powered with location intelligence and geospatial data display demographic segmentation, geographic trends, and hyper-local insights.

Data Visualization

Identify and share real-time trends and new insights derived from numbers, interactive graphics, and easy-to-understand charts and graphs, making data more accessible and engaging for users.

First-Party Data

Scrape, clean, and organize data with data acquisition tools and build your own first-party data program.

Who uses Meradesh.org

Investment Houses

Investment banks and firms use Meradesh.org to gain insights on economic indicators and political developments and use data visualization tools to conduct comprehensive market analysis and identify investment opportunities.

FMCG firms

The platform offers FMCG firms data-driven insights into economic conditions, consumer sentiment, and market trends, enabling them to more effectively manage supply chains, develop new products, and execute marketing campaigns.

Entrepreneurs

Smaller organizations and startups use Meradesh.org's machine learning and data acquisition tools to make informed decisions by employing computational statistics for market research.
