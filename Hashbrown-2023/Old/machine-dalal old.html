Online Marketplace for Print & Graphics Machinery

Helping people list or find used print and graphic machinery for their printing and packaging businesses. Further, you can directly connect with industry’s leading sellers or advertise your machinery for sale at Machine Dalal with No Commission charged.

Features at Glance

Find New/Used Machinery

Find used sheet feed, offset, pre-press, converting, web, cutters, digital press and ]packaging machines from reputable manufacturers, dealers and sellers.

List Used Machinery

List used print & graphic machines for sale at Machine Dalal to get great exposure and expand your reach. No obligation to share contact details or pricing.

Connect Directly with Sellers

Interested buyers can send their enquiry directly to sellers. Sellers, on other hand, can share their contact details, product price etc. with interested buyers in private.

Post Your Requirement

Searching for a particular machine? Simply post your requirement as a ‘Wanted’ ad here at Machine Dalal to grab attention of Industry’s best sellers

Advertise Your Machinery

Display your used machine as a featured product at our home page. It will help you get maximum viewership, boost sales and reach potential buyers quickly.

List New Machinery

You can list your own production or any new print or graphic machine at Machine Dalal. It can be done using our mobile app only.

Why Machine Dalal

We have the most up-to- date classified database of print and graphics machines for sale, offered by the industry’s leading sellers. This makes Machine Dalal the go-to place for finding and selling print & graphic machines online from brands like Heidelberg, Goss, Manroland, etc.

We don’t let any machine listing go live without verification. Every listing made by any seller/manufacturer is thoroughly checked to confirm machine specifications using various online sources. It helps us keeping our database up-to- date and

Machine Dalal does not buy or sell any machinery- our only mission is to help our clients to do so. We do not charge any commission from sellers or buyers on sale of any machinery. In fact, manufacturers and sellers do not have to pay us any fee for listing their print machines as well.

Stuck anywhere or have a query? We are just an email away. We will be happy to help.
