Leverage The Growing Market of Location-based Services

Stand out amidst the growing customer expectations and competition with Location-based services.

Business Intelligence | Jan 24 2023
By Prakrati Kachroo

Location-based Advertising, Business Intelligence and Analytics, Fleet Management, Mapping and Navigation, Social Networking and Entertainment, Proximity Marketing, Asset Tracking, you name it.   

The undisputed need to get in on the growing market is evident-  

The global location-based services market size grew from $70.27 Billion in 2022 to $88.42 Billion in 2023 at the compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 25.8%. And the market size is expected to grow to $226.16 Billion in 2027 at a CAGR of 26.5%. 

Over time, the importance of location has only grown, and with that, the ways it is leveraged. 'Location' is now more of an asset than ever owing to technologies, IoT and geo-analytics. 

Yes, we're talking Location Intelligence and further on Location-based Services. The location and everything about it, down to the last block. For delivering location-specific, personalized information, entertainment, or services. 

You must have used Google Maps to navigate, Uber to get to your destination, Foursquare to find a nearby restaurant or played Pokémon Go. All these are unique examples Location-based services. And these aren't the only ones. With the growing need and increasing competition, location-based services are being tapped into by myriad industries for varied applications. 

 
As the stats suggest, More and More Businesses like yours are now embracing Location-based Services. And Here's why you should too:  

Improve Customer Experience: With LBS, you can offer their customers relevant content and services. Additionally, you can offer more convenience. 

Receive Detailed Insights into Customer Behavior: LBS can help you understand your customers' buying patterns. 

Maximizing Marketing Efforts: You can leverage location-specific targeting to reach the right audience. You can notify customers near your location about offers. 

Location-based services make it easier for businesses to build, offer and market products and services.
