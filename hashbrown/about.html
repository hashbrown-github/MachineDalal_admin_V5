“If I have seen further,” Isaac Newton wrote in a 1675 letter to fellow scientist Robert Hooke, “it is by standing on the shoulders of giants.”

Standing on the shoulders of giants is a necessary part of creativity, innovation, and development at Hashbrown Systems. Our approach, our values and our management ideals are drawn from the greatest and nothing less we believe should be offered to our clients.

The People-

Our team of dedicated designers, application programmers, data scientists and analysts are men and women of scientific rigour. What makes them extraordinary is the amount of indispensable tacit knowledge they have gathered over the years working with an array of industries- Fintech, Location Intelligence and Cloud Computing to name a dominant few.

The Values-

The core values that define us are intelligence and honesty. The core does not change. Markets evolve, technology evolves, and people come and go, but the core of this organization does not change.

We value intelligence for the simple reason that it helps us separate fads from essentials.

Our intelligent approach to development allows us to provide solutions that are cost-effective, resilient and sturdy. We don’t get swayed by hype, but critically look at the best way to provide our customers with effective solutions.

Why is honesty important to us?

For the very simple reason that it is the necessary foundation for a sustainable and long-standing relationship.

Our Management Approach-

“If you put fences around people, you get sheep. Give the people the room they need.” — William L. McKnight, Chairman- 3M

At Hashbrown Systems, we go hard on mistakes, but not on people. We encourage taking initiative, experimentation and taking risks- primarily key ingredients for innovation.  Creating a psychologically safe space and working process that reinforces learning from mistakes provides Hashbrown Systems with an enviable advantage. 

Mission-

Our team, values and management style have evolved over the years to accomplish one goal- to provide our clients with a market-centric competitive advantage.

This remains Hashbrown Systems’ ultimate goal and purpose.
