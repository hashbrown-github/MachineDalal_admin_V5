Overcome Operational Challenges in Running OOH Campaigns

Streamline your Outdoor Campaign Management with Hashbrown's Out-of-Home Auditing and Monitoring System.

Prakrati Kachroo
18 / 08 / 2023

OOH advertising is an integral part of an advertising mix, providing a unique opportunity to extend brand visibility, increase awareness, and reinforce marketing campaigns by capturing attention in high-traffic locations, delivering a powerful and memorable message.

However, managing and executing an OOH campaign comes with its own set of operational challenges. From ensuring the quality and placement of advertisements to tracking campaign performance, advertisers often face difficulties in achieving their objectives. And this is where the need for a comprehensive OOH system arises- An end-to-end auditing and monitoring system that allows you to overcome operational challenges and run multiple campaigns in multiple locations smoothly, one like Hashbrown's OOH auditing and monitoring system that addresses these challenges effectively.

Addressing Overcoming Operational Challenges

Managing Large-scale Campaigns: Coordinating and managing numerous billboards and assets across multiple cities can be a daunting task. It requires efficient logistics, communication, and coordination to ensure that all advertisements are deployed correctly and on time.

  • Ensuring Quality and Compliance: Maintaining the quality of advertisements and ensuring compliance with brand guidelines is crucial for the success of an OOH campaign. Challenges arise when there are inconsistencies in ad placement, tampering, or unauthorized changes made to the creative.
  • Tracking and Measuring Performance: Measuring the effectiveness of an OOH campaign can be challenging without proper tracking mechanisms. Obtaining real-time data on impressions, audience reach, and engagement becomes difficult, making it harder to evaluate campaign performance accurately.

  • On-Ground Reporting: With our reporting capabilities, Hashbrown's system provides advertisers with up-to-date data on campaign performance, impressions, and audience reach. This enables them to make informed decisions and optimize their campaigns on the go.
